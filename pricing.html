Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pricing and services.

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the beginning of your next billing cycle.

What's included in the SLA?

Our SLA covers system uptime, performance metrics, and response times. Enterprise plans include custom SLA agreements.

Do you offer annual discounts?

Yes, we offer 15% annual discounts and custom enterprise pricing for long-term commitments.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees for Starter and Professional plans. Enterprise implementations may include one-time setup costs.
