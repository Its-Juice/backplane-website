About Backplane

We are dedicated to transforming enterprise infrastructure through innovative technology solutions and expert consulting services.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Backplane emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to make enterprise-grade infrastructure accessible to businesses of all sizes. Our founders, seasoned veterans from leading tech companies, recognized that many organizations were struggling with outdated infrastructure that couldn't keep pace with modern digital demands.

What started as a small consulting firm has grown into a comprehensive infrastructure solutions provider, serving over 500 companies worldwide. We've maintained our core principle throughout this growth: every business deserves access to reliable, scalable, and secure infrastructure solutions.

Today, Backplane stands at the forefront of infrastructure innovation, helping organizations navigate the complexities of cloud adoption, security modernization, and digital transformation. Our team combines deep technical expertise with a genuine commitment to client success.
